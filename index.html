Helping others with your
computer

A computer is a vital tool for anyone living in this day and age.

This is why we aim to donate every 1 in 10 computers we collect

to charitable and worthy causes.

Safe & Secure

We ensure all previously stored

information on any of the systems

we collect is either removed, given

to you, or destroyed.

Eco-Friendly

We aim to reuse all that we collect

from you. reducing wastage and

the manufacture of new systems

has a range of benefit for our

future on this planet.

Helping Others

We donate 1 in 10 the machines

to charities and worthy causes.

This helps grow individuals who

are in need of the benefits a

computer can bring.

Welcome to We Collect your IT

We offer a free collection service across the UK for the removal of your old IT systems. We safely reuse this

equipment by ensuring any sensitive or personal information that was on these machines is either physically

removed or destroyed before we reuse them. Using us shall not only ebnefit the environment by avoiding the

waste and disposal of this equipment but shall also help others, as we donate and proportion of all the

equipment we collect
